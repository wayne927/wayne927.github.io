? About Wayne Ngan

I am a computational scientist living in Toronto, Ontario. I grew up in Hong Kong and moved to Vancouver with my family when I was twelve. After finishing my undergraduate studies in the University of British Columbia, I moved to Toronto to pursue a PhD in computational astrophysics at the University of Toronto. I currently work as a developer for a database software.

Scientific interests

I am interested in writing code to solve numerical problems in general. For my PhD thesis, I focused on the computational aspect of astronomy where I used numerical simulations to simulate astrophysical systems. Both the simulations and the analysis were too large to fit into a single computer, so the code had to be run in parallel in high-performance computing facilities. The results helped us understand how to use the dynamics of stars to probe the distribution of dark matter in our galaxy.
